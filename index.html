What We Treat

Specialist Services for Complete Recovery

Advanced laser-based procedures with minimal pain, rapid healing, and lasting results — all under one specialist roof.

Piles (Haemorrhoids) Treatment
Laser haemorrhoidoplasty for internal and external piles. Near-painless, no cuts, no stitches — back to normal within days.
Learn More
Fissure Treatment
Laser sphincterotomy to heal anal fissures permanently. Resolves chronic pain and bleeding with superior long-term outcomes.
Learn More
Fistula Treatment
VAAFT & laser fistulotomy to precisely close fistula tracts without damaging surrounding tissue or muscles.
Learn More
Pilonidal Sinus Treatment
Laser-assisted sinus obliteration with excellent recurrence-free rates and minimal downtime compared to open surgery.
Learn More
Day Care Procedures
Arrive in the morning, head home by afternoon. Our streamlined day-care model ensures safe, efficient surgical care with no overnight stay needed.
