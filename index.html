What We Treat

Specialized Services

Advanced, minimally invasive procedures for lasting anorectal health.

Piles (Hemorrhoids)

Internal and external hemorrhoid treatment using advanced laser ablation for painless, rapid recovery with minimal downtime.

  • Laser hemorrhoidoplasty
  • No cuts, no stitches
  • Same-day discharge
Most Common

Anal Fissure

Effective treatment of painful anal tears through non-surgical and laser methods that promote healing and relieve chronic discomfort.

  • Lateral sphincterotomy
  • Laser fissurectomy
  • Dietary guidance included
High Success Rate

Anal Fistula

Precision laser treatment of fistula-in-ano that seals the abnormal tract without damaging the sphincter muscle — preserving continence.

  • FiLaC (Laser closure)
  • Sphincter preservation
  • Low recurrence rate
Sphincter Safe

Pilonidal Sinus

Modern pit-picking and laser techniques to treat pilonidal sinus disease with significantly faster healing and minimal scarring.

  • Laser pit picking
  • Minimal wound care
  • Quick return to routine
Minimal Scarring

Meet Your Doctor

Expert in Laser Proctology

Dr. Sufiya Adil Shaikh is a highly experienced General Surgeon with a specialization in laser proctology.
